(10) Intensity [Nature]
-----------------------

A ten player map designed for 
intense 5v5 battles.  Resources in 
the starting points are fairly 
limited, so early expansion must 
be considered a must.  Resource 
fields outwith the starting zones 
are very much exposed, so expansions 
will be difficult to defend.

Size: Huge  
Tunnels: No 
Author: NatureX
